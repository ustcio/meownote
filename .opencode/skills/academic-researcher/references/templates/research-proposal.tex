% ============================================================
\section{Abstract / Executive Summary}
% ============================================================

Provide a concise overview (200--300 words) of the proposed research:
the problem, approach, expected outcomes, and significance.

% ============================================================
\section{Introduction and Background}
% ============================================================

Describe the research area and its importance. Summarize the current
state of knowledge and cite key prior work \citep{smith2023,jones2022}.
Clearly identify the gap or problem that motivates this proposal.

% ============================================================
\section{Research Questions and Hypotheses}
% ============================================================

State the specific research questions or hypotheses:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{RQ1:} What is the effect of X on Y?
  \item \textbf{RQ2:} How does Z mediate the relationship between X and Y?
  \item \textbf{H1:} We hypothesize that X leads to improved Y under condition Z.
\end{enumerate}

% ============================================================
\section{Proposed Methodology}
% ============================================================

\subsection{Research Design}
Describe the overall research design (experimental, quasi-experimental,
qualitative, mixed methods, etc.).

\subsection{Data Collection}
Describe data sources, participants, instruments, and sampling strategy.

\subsection{Analysis Plan}
Describe the analytical methods and tools to be used.

\subsection{Ethical Considerations}
Note any IRB/ethics board requirements and how they will be addressed.
